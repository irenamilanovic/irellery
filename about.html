How it started...

Our story begins way back in 1886 and continues through the centuries thanks to the main inspiration – the love for jewelry. The knowledge, skills and pursuit of mastery of hand-crafted jewelry have been passed down from fathers to sons and daughters, receiving striking artistic expression through the Irellery brand.

The very beginning is connected to a reputable family jewelry store in Thessaloniki, where the wonderful love story of a young couple directed the further development and growth of the brand. Talent, ambition and creativity lead Irellery to the status of the most famous Belgrade and Yugoslavian goldsmith of the last century, respected even by world jewelers.
